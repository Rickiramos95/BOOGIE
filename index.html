Artist Profile

A Boogie
Wit Da Hoodie

Highbridge, Bronx, NY

Straight out of Highbridge, The Bronx—A Boogie blurred every line between melody, street rap, and pop to build a lane nobody else can touch. Six albums deep, a #1 Billboard 200 debut with Hoodie SZN, the youngest New York artist to go Diamond, and a spot on the Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse soundtrack—he's still just getting started. In February 2026 he sold out Radio City Music Hall in six minutes flat and dropped Before Artistry, celebrating a decade of dominance.
